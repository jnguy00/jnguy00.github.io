Staff-led Programs

The Campus Y seeks to enrich and expand upon the education and experiences of Carolina students. Every year, the Y supports approximately 4,000 students to use their advocacy, education, service, and innovation skills in both local and foreign communities. Students have the opportunity to participate in any of our three application-based, staff-led programs.
